# High-Risk Feature Verification

## Normal Pulse Users
Normal users are not required to submit government ID, selfie verification, address documents, resumes, or sensitive personal files at signup.

## Paid Teachers
Recommended requirements:
- Email verified
- Phone verified
- ID verification
- Selfie verification
- Teacher agreement
- Credentials or portfolio review where applicable

## Marketplace Sellers
Recommended requirements:
- Email verified
- Phone verified
- Payment account verified
- Seller policy acceptance

## Creator Monetization and Payouts
Recommended requirements:
- Payment account verified
- Tax/compliance workflow where legally required
- Identity verification where required by payment provider

## Admins and Moderators
Recommended requirements:
- Two-factor authentication required
- Strong password required
- Recent reauthentication for sensitive actions
