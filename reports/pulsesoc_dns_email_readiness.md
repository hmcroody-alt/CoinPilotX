## SPF note

There is exactly one public SPF record for `pulsesoc.com`, and it is generated by Namecheap Private Email. Brevo did not require an SPF record in its PulseSoc domain-authentication checklist. I did not create a second SPF record.

To merge Brevo into SPF, Namecheap's automatic Private Email mail setting may need to be converted into manual/custom mail records. That is higher risk because it can affect mailbox routing. I left Private Email automatic mail settings intact.
