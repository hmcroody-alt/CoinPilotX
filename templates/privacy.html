Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CoinPilotXAI Inc. collects, uses, and protects information when you use CoinPilotX, the CoinPilotX website, Telegram bot, and related services.

Depending on how you use CoinPilotX, we may collect:

The website may use basic cookies, browser storage, server logs, or analytics tools to understand site performance, product interest, and conversion flow. Analytics are intended for product improvement and should not include private keys, seed phrases, or wallet passwords.

CoinPilotX operates through Telegram. Telegram may process information under its own privacy practices. CoinPilotXAI Inc. uses Telegram information only to operate bot commands, account status, subscriptions, alerts, and user-requested intelligence features.

Public Blockchain Data

CoinPilotX may analyze public blockchain data such as public wallet addresses and transaction IDs. Public blockchain data is not private. Never submit seed phrases, private keys, recovery phrases, wallet passwords, or signing credentials.

  • To improve reliability, safety, product quality, and customer support.
  • To detect abuse, protect users, and maintain service security.

Security Practices

CoinPilotXAI Inc. uses reasonable technical and organizational safeguards for stored data. No online system can be guaranteed perfectly secure. CoinPilotXAI Inc. will never ask for seed phrases, private keys, recovery phrases, wallet passwords, or remote wallet access.
